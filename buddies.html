Buddies

Sudoku variant · ✦ hard
Normal Sudoku: fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with 1–9.
Buddies: every row and column has a clue at both ends, read inward. The first cell from that end is the indicator. Its two buddies are the digits one higher and one lower than it — and the values wrap, so 1's buddies are 2 and 9. The clue is the sum of the cells lying between the two buddies (a clue of 0 means the buddies are adjacent).
